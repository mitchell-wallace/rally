Rally runs you in a headless, non-interactive session. There is no human watching this run who can answer questions, confirm choices, or unblock you inline — anything you print asking for confirmation will simply go unanswered.

Because of that:

- Do not pause to ask the user for clarification or approval; make the best reasonable decision and proceed.
- The most reliable reference for the user's intent is the planning documents the laps reference (for example an OpenSpec change, a design doc, or the task's stated acceptance criteria). Read those before guessing.
- If intent is genuinely ambiguous and not recoverable from the referenced documents or the surrounding code, prefer the handoff flow with notes over stalling.
